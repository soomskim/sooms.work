Joyce Florist

2020-2021

visual identity, social media

Joyce Florist is a local flower shop located in Tenafly, NJ. The head florist, Sophia, had been working with flowers for over thirty years and managing the shop since 2000. Revitalized visual identity, designed promotional materials, and started/ran an Instagram account for a local flower shop. All to better affirm the small business's lasting quality in flowers, customer relationships, and presence within its local community.

I started an Instagram account for the shop to 1✿ promote the business and maintain engagement with the local community (as we were in the first year of the pandemic) and 2✿ archive Sophia's work, plant tips, and moments informed by years of labor and craft.

I photographed content & managed the account until Dec. 2021.
