Joyce Florist

2020-current

visual identity, social media, website design

Joyce Florist is a local flower shop located in Tenafly, NJ that has been working with flowers and plants since 2000. As a freelance designer, I revitalized their visual identity, designed promotional materials, and started/ran an Instagram account for a local flower shop. All to better affirm the small business's lasting quality in flowers, customer relationships, and presence within its local community.

I'm currently designing an e-commerce website to optimize customer shopping experience; increase flexibility; and allow the florists more control and flexibility.

I started an Instagram account for the shop to 1✿ promote the business and maintain engagement with the local community (as we were in the first year of the pandemic) and 2✿ archive Sophia's work, plant tips, and moments informed by years of labor and craft.

I photographed content & managed the account until Dec. 2021.
