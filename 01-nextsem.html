NextSem

2018, Wellesley College, CS220: Human-Computer Interaction

UI/UX, mobile interface design

NextSem is a mobile application that seeks to solve chronic painpoints in Wellesley College's biannual course selection process. At the heart of this project lies a desire to provide ease and empowerment for Wellesley students planning for their undergrad careers.

Advisor: Orit Shaer
Team: Christina Chen, Hiya Vazirani

Currently, students have to navigate through disjointed platforms to see their major requirements, distribution requirements, and the course browser. Most singly balance needs and interests through external support such as excel sheets, written notes, and scheduling apps; adding yet another platform to the process.

NextSem consolidates it all in ☼ one place ☼ for higher-quality support, convenience, and experience. With this app, students can login and access information regarding their personal requirements, the course browser, as well as calendars for one to personalize and draft future semesters.
